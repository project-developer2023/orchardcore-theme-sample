Vehicle Syndication

As a business owner, managing inventory across multiple locations can be a time-consuming and challenging process. Fortunately, Elastic Dealer's Vehicle Syndication feature can help simplify and streamline this process. By consolidating all of your vehicle sources into one central system, this feature allows you to easily manage and track inventory from multiple locations. With the ability to extract inventory data from your existing DMS, database, or files, Elastic Dealer's Vehicle Syndication feature offers a convenient and efficient way to maintain inventory and expose it online.
